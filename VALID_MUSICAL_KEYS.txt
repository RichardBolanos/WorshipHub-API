========================================
WORSHIPHUB API - VALID MUSICAL KEYS
========================================

REGEX PATTERN:
^[A-G][#b]?(m|maj|min|sus2|sus4|add9|7|maj7|min7|dim|aug)?$

========================================
EXAMPLES OF VALID KEYS:
========================================
C       - C major
G       - G major
Am      - A minor
F#      - F sharp major
Bb      - B flat major
Ebm     - E flat minor
Gmaj    - G major (explicit)
Dmin    - D minor (explicit)
C#m     - C sharp minor
Abmaj   - A flat major
G7      - G dominant seventh
Cmaj7   - C major seventh
Amin7   - A minor seventh
F#7     - F sharp dominant seventh
Dsus4   - D suspended fourth
Asus2   - A suspended second
Gadd9   - G added ninth
Bdim    - B diminished
C#aug   - C sharp augmented

========================================
INVALID EXAMPLES:
========================================
H       - Invalid note (only A-G)
C##     - Double sharp not allowed
Cbb     - Double flat not allowed
c       - Lowercase not allowed
CM      - Uppercase M not allowed
Cma     - Invalid modifier
C9      - 9 alone not allowed (use add9)
Cmaj9   - maj9 not in pattern
